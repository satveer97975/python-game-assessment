SNAKE GAME INSTRUCTIONS

Objective:
- Control the snake to eat the red food
- Grow as long as possible without hitting walls or yourself

Controls:
- UP ARROW: Move up
- DOWN ARROW: Move down
- LEFT ARROW: Move left
- RIGHT ARROW: Move right
- Q: Quit game (when game over)
- C: Play again (when game over)

Scoring:
- Each food eaten: +1 point
- High score is saved automatically
